About Us

The Electrical Engineering Society ,IIT Kharagpur is a link between the faculty and the students of the Department of Electrical Engineering. It is the body, responsible for carrying out the various welfare programs for the students of the department, and to publicize about the opportunities and achievements regarding the students and faculties. The society is made up of a varied collection of individuals who are working together to organize events such as field trips, freshers' welcome, farewell, guest lectures, and interactive sessions.

Events

Fresher's Welcome

The orientation cum welcome event for freshers was organized for giving overall idea about their upcoming experiences and expectations from the department.

Industrial Trips

Orientation of the new batch towards department activities and their mutual interaction.

Interactive Sessions

EES has organised various interactive sessions for the students sitting for placements and also for those who were trying for internships.

Competitions

Small theme based events where students team up to have fun, scratch their heads over trivial puzzles, quizzes, sports or just a minute games…!!

Teachers Day

Teachers' Day is celebrated as a tribute to the contribution made by teachers to the society by imparting knowledge and enlightening the students.

Sports

Various sports activities like footer, cricket etc is carried out throughout the year, between different batches of students.

Alumni Visit

Alumni from silver jubilee , golden jubilee batches, visit the Department while attending the Alumni Meet. A good opportunity for us students to interact with the Alumni , know their life experiences.

Farewell

The felicitation of the outgoing batch who takes a walk down the memory lane, as we bid them adieus.

Workshops

Various workshops such as on MATLAB are also conducted to provide the students a platform to build their skills.
